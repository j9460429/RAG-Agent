## What is NexusMind?

NexusMind is an AI-powered intelligent knowledge management platform. Its core philosophy is to make your knowledge "come alive." After uploading documents, AI doesn't just store them — it understands, connects, and proactively provides insights when you need them.

## Core Value

- **Smart Knowledge Base**: Upload files in any format, AI automatically parses, embeds, and creates knowledge graphs
- **RAG-Enhanced Chat**: AI automatically references your knowledge base, ensuring grounded and traceable answers
- **🧠 Dual-Layer Memory System**: Automatically learns preferences and facts, making the AI understand you better over time
- **🎭 Settings & Personas**: Rich AI role switching and custom persona management
- **🎨 Canvas & Diagrams**: AI generates documents and edits interactive flowcharts and mind maps via Cytoscape
- **🛠️ Isolated Skill Sandbox**: Executes code in a secure Docker container to generate in-depth reports and files
- **Telegram Bot**: Always-available AI interaction on your mobile device
